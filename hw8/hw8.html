College and University Proximity Map

Methodology

This map answers the question, “what portions of North Carolina do and do not have relative access to a degree-granting college or university in their geographic proximity?” To answer this question, I utilized a dataset of all universities and colleges in North Carolina overlayed on polygons containing North Carolina and all her counties. I first created a 15-mile buffer around the points of all universities and colleges. I then took the difference of the North Carolina county polygon and the buffers to see the areas lying outside of the buffer. These areas in the difference polygon are the ones without relative proximity to a college or university, and the other areas in the other polygon do.
